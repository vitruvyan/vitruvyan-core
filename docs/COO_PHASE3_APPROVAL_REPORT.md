## 🎯 Project Overview

**Objective:** Transform Vitruvyan-Core from finance-specific to domain-agnostic architecture, enabling multi-vertical market expansion while maintaining full backward compatibility.

**Scope:** Complete refactoring of VEE (Explainability), VARE (Risk), and VWRE (Attribution) engines to accept domain-specific providers instead of hardcoded finance logic.

**Business Value:** Enable Vitruvyan-Core deployment across healthcare, logistics, manufacturing, and other verticals, creating new revenue streams and market opportunities.

### ✅ Technical Deliverables Completed
- **3 Abstract Contracts Created:** ExplainabilityContract, RiskContract, AggregationContract
- **3 Finance Providers Implemented:** Domain-specific implementations for backward compatibility
- **3 Core Engines Refactored:** VEE, VARE, VWRE now accept domain providers
- **Database Schema Updated:** From 'entity_id' to 'entity_id' for domain agnosticism
